Key features

Everything you need to understand where you stand today and what to learn next – without guessing.

AI-powered resume analysis

The system reads your resume, extracts your current skills, tools and education automatically.

02 · Skill gaps
Find missing skills instantly

03 · Personalized plan
Adaptive learning roadmap

How it works

Simple 3-step flow for students, freshers and working professionals.

Step 1
Create your account

Sign up using email or your institute login and share basic details about your goals.

Step 2
Upload resume or skills

Upload your PDF resume or enter your skills manually. The AI identifies strengths and missing areas.

Step 3
Follow your learning path

Receive a personalized roadmap, track progress and chat with the learning assistant whenever you’re stuck.
